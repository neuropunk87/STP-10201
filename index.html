Journey Home – Merge & Stories

Dive into the charming town of Lake Arbos, where every merge brings you closer to solving family mysteries and restoring beautiful spaces. Team up with Rachel and Detective Collins, combine everyday items into delightful creations, and give your café and home a personal touch — all while meeting quirky locals and uncovering secrets hidden around every corner.

About the game

Journey Home - Merge & Stories is a cozy and mysterious merge puzzle game where you help Rachel uncover hidden family secrets, renovate charming spaces, and serve treats in your cafe — all while exploring a small town full of quirky characters and dramatic gossip.

The game blends an easy-to-learn yet addictive mechanic: merge everyday items (like eggs, jam, coffee, and more) to create fresh recipes for the Local Crust Café — your central hub for interacting with locals and progressing through the story. These combinations not only help you serve customers but also unlock new categories of items and resources to keep the gameplay evolving.

How to play

  • Explore Locations – Visit the café, home, garden, and mansion to find new tasks.
  • Merge Items – Combine 3+ identical objects to create upgraded ones.
  • Complete Tasks – Fulfill quests to unlock story chapters and rewards.
  • Decorate & Renovate – Use earned resources to upgrade and style locations.
  • Talk to Characters – Interact with locals to get missions and discover secrets.
  • Use Boosters – Speed up merging or overcome tricky challenges.
  • Join Events – Participate in seasonal or limited-time activities for special prizes.
